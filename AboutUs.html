Hii! Welcome


This Project Hostel Complaint Management System is designed to simplify the process of lodging and resolving complaints within hostel premises. Whether it's maintenance issues or general hostel grievances, our system ensures QUICK and TRANSPARANT complaint handling.




Purpose of this project

Managing complains manually can be slow and inefficient. Our System streamline this process by submitting complains to submit complains online which helps hostel adminsiarators to track complains and fix the complain in timely manner. This system also keep track of delay of respond to a complain which helps rate the hostel services. We aim to enhance hostel living conditions by improving communication between students and authorities




Features

1. Easy complain submission

2. Status Tracking

3. Admin Panal

4. Notification and Alerts

5. Secure and User-Friendly
